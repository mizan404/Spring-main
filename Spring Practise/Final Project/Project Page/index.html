Hospital Management Information System

A hospital management information system (HMIS) is an element of
health informatics that focuses mainly on the administration needs of
hospitals. In many implementations, this is a comprehensive, integrated
information system designed to manage all the aspects of a hospital. One
of the most important issues is health care services.
Hospital management information system (HMIS) is a province-wide
initiative designed to improve access to patient information through a
central electronic information system. HIS goal is to streamline patient
information flow and its accessibility for doctors and other health care
providers. These changes in service will improve patient care quality and
patient safety over time.
The patient carries system record patient information, patient laboratory
test results, and patient doctor information. Doctors can access easily
person information, test results, and previous prescriptions. Patient
schedule organization and early warning systems can be provided by
related systems.
